Master Minds
Quiz

4

Quiz

  • Rules
  • Register
  • General Instructions :

  • This is a team-based competition in which students have to form a team of 2 members.
  • The competition is divided into 2 rounds i.e. - 1. Screening Round 2. Final Round.
  • In screening round only one participant from the team can participate.
  • The Questions will be from the fields related to General knowledge, Management Science, Biological Science, Journalism, Mass communication and Computer Science.

  • Rules for Screening :

  • A questionnaire consisting of 20 questions (general studies related questions) will be given to the Students.
  • Time Limit- 10 minutes.
  • Selection- Best 5 teams will be selected on the basis of their score.
  • There will be negative marking for the wrong answer.

  • Rules for Final Round :

  • There will be 4 rounds.
  • First round will be based on Multiple Choice Questions.
  • Second Round (Audio Visual Round) - In this an image will be displayed on the projector & teams have to identify the image and answer the question related to them.
  • Third Round (Rapid fire Round)- In this round, teams have to answer questions in given time.
  • Fourth Round (Buzzer round).
